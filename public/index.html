Whenever you are booking, we will always give you some instructions and advice on what you will need to pick up the equipment, but here is a short answer you may want to know prior to booking.

Most residential equpment pieces, like residential bouncy castles, regular food machines, and small arcade games are designed to fit into the trunk or interior of a small sedan or a hatchback. So, anything from a size of Nissan Micra or a Mini Cooper will be pretty much enough for a couple of equipment pieces.

Larger equipment, like commercial bounncy castles, commercial food machines, ice cream machines, and bumper cars is not going to fit in small cars mentioned above, so depending on the size of your booking, we would suggest coming in an SUV or a pick-up truck.

For other oversized equipment, like jumbo castles, or just large orders, we may suggest to order delivery or come in a van or a larger truck to fit it all in.

The installation process for both residential and commercial bouncy castles is super straightforward. You just need to follow these three simple steps:

1. Find a smooth plain spot in the place you plan to install the bouncy castle. Make sure the ground is clear of debris and sharp objects.
2. Roll out the bouncy castle from the bag and stretch it into a rectangular form using its' corners.
3. Hook up the air pump to the air funnel and turn it on.

You are all set! Now wait for 5 minutes and your bouncy castle is ready to have fun on.

You can do all the steps above all by yourself for both residential and commercial bouncy castles.
But we would suggest you use some help for the commercial castles as they are bigger and heavier, so you might risk injuring yourself if you do it alone.

Our machines are super easy to operate, even if it is your first time, so you can do it all yourself.

There are some specifics things to know before you operate certain machines, so we are going to show you operation steps and general rules when you come for the pick up.

Also, after you pick up the equipment, we are going to send you an email with file containing pictured instructions for your machine.

Depending on the type of the equipment you are renting you will be charged a small security deposit amount when you come for the pick up of the equipment.

The amount of security deposit you will be required to pay will vary depending on the size and total price of your booking.

Once the equipment is returned and inspected the deposit sum will be reimbursed to you immediately.

You are never going to be charged for any damage which falls under the cateory of equipment wear and tear. Here are all the damages, which fall under wear&tear category:

1. Discolouring and/or paint cracking on any equipment.
2. Equipment loosing power or battery life time, because of regular use.
3. Change in visual appeal of equipment caused by regular use.
4. Equipment malfunction(s), caused by production mistake.

And here are the damages, which would NOT fall under wear&tear category, and applicable charges:

1. Discolouring and/or paint cracking on any equipment, caused by improper use and/or transportation - 20% of the security deposit.
2. Exposing equipment to harsh weather conditions, unless allowed by equipmen instructions - up to 30% of the security deposit.
3. Change in visual appeal of equipment caused by food, beverage, and/or blood stains - up to 30% of the security deposit.
4. Equipment loosing power or battery life time, caused by use of unsuitable charger/power cords - 50% of the security deposit.
5. Complete destruction or return of equipment, which can no longer be used or rented out - up to 100% of the security deposit.
